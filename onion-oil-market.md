<p><ul><li>Steam Distillation</li><li>Solvent Extraction</li><li>Supercritical Extraction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Onion oil is extracted using various methods, each impacting the quality and market type. Steam distillation involves heating onions to extract essential oils, favored for its purity. Solvent extraction employs chemical agents to dissolve oils, offering higher yields but potentially affecting quality. Supercritical extraction uses carbon dioxide under high pressure to produce a high-quality oil without solvent residues, appealing for premium markets. Each method serves different consumer preferences and market demands, influencing pricing and applications in food, cosmetics, and aromatherapy.</p></p>
