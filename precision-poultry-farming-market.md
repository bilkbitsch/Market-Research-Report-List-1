<p><p>Precision Poultry Farming encompasses two primary market types: Free Caged Poultry Farming and Caged Poultry Farming. Free Caged Poultry Farming allows birds to roam within designated areas while maintaining some enclosure, promoting natural behaviors and potentially enhancing animal welfare. In contrast, Caged Poultry Farming involves housing birds in confined cages, often prioritizing space efficiency and production output. Both systems utilize technology for monitoring health, performance, and productivity, aiming to optimize sustainability and profitability in poultry production.</p></p>
