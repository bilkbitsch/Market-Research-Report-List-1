<p><p>The facial cleanser brush market is categorized into two main types: manual and automatic. Manual facial cleanser brushes rely on user motion for scrubbing and exfoliating the skin, offering a personalized experience and control over pressure and technique. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with manual brushes emphasizing simplicity and tradition, while automatic brushes focus on advanced technology and convenience.</p></p>
