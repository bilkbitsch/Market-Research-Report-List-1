<p><p>The power semiconductor module market is categorized into four main types: diodes, transistors, thyristors, and others. Diodes allow current to flow in one direction, providing essential rectification in power systems. Transistors serve as switches or amplifiers, controlling power flow efficiently. Thyristors enable high-voltage and high-current control, often used in industrial applications. The 'others' category may include various specialized devices, like IGBTs and MOSFETs, catering to specific applications in renewable energy, automotive, and industrial electronics.</p></p>
