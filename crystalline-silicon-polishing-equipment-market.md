<p><p>The Crystalline Silicon Polishing Equipment market is categorized into two primary types: single-chip polishing and batch polishing. Single-chip polishing focuses on processing individual silicon wafers, offering high precision and control, ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. In contrast, batch polishing involves polishing multiple wafers simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and throughput, making it suitable for high-volume production. Both methods aim to achieve the necessary surface quality required for optimal performance in solar cells and semiconductor devices.</p></p>
