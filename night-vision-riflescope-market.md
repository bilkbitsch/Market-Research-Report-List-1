<p><p>The Night Vision Riflescope market is primarily segmented into two types: thermal imaging and other night vision technologies. Thermal imaging scopes detect heat emitted from objects, allowing for visibility in complete darkness and through obscurants like fog and smoke. This technology is ideal for hunting and tactical operations. The 'other' category includes traditional night vision devices that amplify ambient light, making them suitable for various nighttime activities, but they may struggle in total darkness compared to thermal models.</p></p>
