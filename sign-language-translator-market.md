<p><p>The Sign Language Translator market encompasses three main types: Speech Recognition, Camera Recognition, and Contact Identification. Speech Recognition involves converting spoken language into sign language through algorithms, enhancing communication for the hearing impaired. Camera Recognition uses image processing to analyze and translate signed gestures captured through video, facilitating real-time interpretation. Contact Identification employs wearable technology or sensors to detect and interpret signs, providing direct interaction and feedback. Together, these technologies aim to bridge communication gaps, promoting inclusivity and accessibility.</p></p>
