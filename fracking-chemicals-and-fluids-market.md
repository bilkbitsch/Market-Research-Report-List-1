<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Oil-Gel Based</li><li>Foam-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fracking chemicals and fluids market consists of three primary types: water-based, oil-gel based, and foam-based fluids. Water-based fluids are widely used for their cost-effectiveness and efficiency in carrying proppants. Oil-gel based fluids provide better lubrication and are suitable for high-temperature wells, enhancing recovery rates. Foam-based fluids combine gas and liquid to reduce water usage and improve fluid mobility, making them ideal for challenging environments where minimizing water impact is crucial. Each type is tailored to specific drilling conditions.</p></p>
