<p><ul><li>Transcatheter Heart Valve</li><li>Tissue Heart Valve</li><li>Mechanical Heart Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aortic heart valve market consists of three primary types: transcatheter heart valves, tissue heart valves, and mechanical heart valves. Transcatheter heart valves are minimally invasive devices used for patients who are at high risk for surgery, allowing for faster recovery. Tissue heart valves, made from biological materials, are preferred for their natural function and reduced risk of thrombosis, while mechanical heart valves, crafted from durable materials, are designed for long-term use but require lifelong anticoagulation therapy to prevent clots.</p></p>
