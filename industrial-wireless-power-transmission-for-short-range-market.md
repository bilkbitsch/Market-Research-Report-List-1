<p><p>Industrial wireless power transmission for short-range applications encompasses several key technologies, notably electromagnetic induction and magnetic field coupling. Electromagnetic induction utilizes magnetic fields generated by alternating currents to transfer energy between coils placed close together, allowing efficient power transfer for devices like electric vehicles and industrial equipment. Magnetic field coupling, on the other hand, uses resonant magnetic fields to enhance efficiency over slightly greater distances, making it suitable for applications requiring reliable power in confined industrial environments while minimizing wiring complexities.</p></p>
