<p><p>The pathogen testing market primarily focuses on methods like PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) and LAMP (Loop-Mediated Isothermal Amplification). PCR is a widely used technique for amplifying DNA, enabling the detection of specific pathogens with high sensitivity and specificity. LAMP, on the other hand, offers a simpler and faster alternative, requiring less expensive equipment and providing results in less than an hour. Both methods are pivotal in clinical diagnostics, food safety, and environmental monitoring, catering to diverse testing needs.</p></p>
