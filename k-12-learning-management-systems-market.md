<p><strong>The K-12 Learning Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based</li><li>On-Premise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>K-12 Learning Management Systems (LMS) are categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based LMS solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers, offering scalability, ease of access, and automatic updates, which are particularly beneficial for schools with limited IT resources. Conversely, on-premise LMS installations require schools to host the system on their own servers, providing greater control over data and customization but necessitating ongoing maintenance and higher upfront costs. Each type caters to different technological capabilities and preferences of educational institutions.</p></p>
