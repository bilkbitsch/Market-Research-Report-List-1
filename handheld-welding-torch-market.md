<p><strong>The Handheld Welding Torch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MIG/MAG Welding Torch</li><li>TIG Welding Torch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld welding torch market is primarily divided into two types: MIG/MAG welding torches and TIG welding torches. MIG/MAG welding torches are designed for metal inert gas and metal active gas welding, providing a continuous feed of wire for efficient, versatile applications. In contrast, TIG welding torches focus on tungsten inert gas welding, allowing for precise control over the weld pool and producing high-quality welds, particularly in thin materials. Both types cater to diverse industrial and professional requirements.</p></p>
