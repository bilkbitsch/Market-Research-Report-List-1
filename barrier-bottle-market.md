<p><p>The Barrier Bottle Market is categorized into two main types: Single Layer and Multi-Layer bottles. Single Layer bottles are made from a single type of material, providing basic protection against oxygen and moisture. In contrast, Multi-Layer bottles combine different materials to enhance barrier properties, offering superior resistance to external elements. Multi-Layer bottles are particularly favored in industries requiring extended product shelf life, while Single Layer bottles are often more cost-effective and suitable for less sensitive products.</p></p>
