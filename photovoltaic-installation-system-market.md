<p><p>The photovoltaic installation system market can be categorized into three main types: rooftop, ground-mounted, and carport systems. Rooftop installations utilize building surfaces to harness solar energy, often in urban settings. Ground-mounted systems are installed on open land, suitable for larger-scale solar farms. Carport installations combine shaded parking structures with solar panels, providing dual functionality by generating energy while protecting vehicles. Each type serves unique spatial and energy needs, contributing to the overall growth of renewable energy sources.</p></p>
