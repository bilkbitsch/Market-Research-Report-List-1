<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dialysis Fluid Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Flux</li><li>High Flux</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis fluid filter market is categorized into low flux and high flux types based on their permeability to solutes and water. Low flux filters have a smaller pore size, effectively removing larger molecules and maintaining electrolyte balance, making them suitable for patients with stable kidney function. In contrast, high flux filters have larger pores, allowing the passage of smaller solutes and facilitating more efficient toxin removal, which is beneficial for patients with more severe kidney impairment and higher fluid overload.</p></p>
