<p><p>The plastic bucket market encompasses various material types, each suited for different applications. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its durability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for industrial use. PP (Polypropylene) offers flexibility and is often used for food storage. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) provides rigidity but is less common in food applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is lightweight and can be used for lighter items. Shrink film pails provide added protection and branding, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
