<p><ul><li>Homecare Peritoneal Dialysis Machine</li><li>Homecare Hemodialysis Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home hemodialysis market consists of two primary types: Homecare Peritoneal Dialysis Machines and Homecare Hemodialysis Machines. Homecare Peritoneal Dialysis Machines use the peritoneal cavity for waste removal through dialysis fluid, allowing patients greater flexibility and independence. In contrast, Homecare Hemodialysis Machines filter blood externally, requiring specific setups and often more intensive monitoring. Both modalities aim to provide convenient treatment options for patients with kidney failure, enhancing their quality of life while reducing the need for frequent clinic visits.</p></p>
