<p><p>The Home Oxygen Therapy Equipment market comprises various types, including oxygen concentrators and oxygen cylinders. Oxygen concentrators extract oxygen from ambient air, providing a continuous supply for patients, often with adjustable settings. In contrast, oxygen cylinders store compressed oxygen in portable canisters, offering a tangible source for patients needing mobility.
