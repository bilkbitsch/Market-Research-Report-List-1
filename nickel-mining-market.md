<p><p>The nickel mining market is primarily divided into two types: sulfide ore and laterite ore. Pentlandite, a key sulfide ore, is primarily mined in regions like Canada and Australia and is valued for its high nickel content and efficient extraction methods. Laterite-nickel ore, found in tropical climates, contains nickel in the form of limonite and saprolite. This ore type requires more complex processing techniques, often leading to higher production costs but crucial for meeting growing global demand for nickel.</p></p>
