<p><p>The Miniature Ball Screw market is categorized mainly into two types: Ground Ball Screws and Rolled Ball Screws. Ground Ball Screws are precision-engineered through grinding processes, offering superior accuracy and a smoother surface finish, ideal for high-precision applications. Conversely, Rolled Ball Screws are created through a rolling process, making them more cost-effective for applications that do not require the utmost precision.
