<p><p>The Ultrasonic Scalpel System market is primarily segmented into two types: gun-type scalpels and scissor-type scalpels. Gun-type scalpels feature a handheld design that allows for precise cutting and coagulation of tissue with minimal thermal damage, making them ideal for various surgical procedures. In contrast, scissor-type scalpels offer enhanced maneuverability and control, facilitating intricate dissection and tissue separation. Both types leverage ultrasonic technology to improve surgical efficiency, reduce bleeding, and enhance patient recovery in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
