<p><p>The veterinary clinical thermometers market includes various types, primarily ear type and anal type thermometers. Ear type thermometers utilize infrared sensors to measure the temperature of a pet through the ear canal, providing quick and non-invasive readings. Anal type thermometers, on the other hand, require insertion into the rectum for more accurate core body temperature measurement, making them reliable but more invasive. Both types are essential for monitoring pet health and diagnosing medical conditions in veterinary practice.</p></p>
