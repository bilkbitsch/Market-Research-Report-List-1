<p><p>A tubular retractor is a surgical instrument designed to access deep tissue structures with minimal trauma to the surrounding area. Additionally, the growing elderly population and advancements in surgical training and education increase awareness and adoption of tubular retractors among healthcare professionals.</p><p>Latest trends indicate a shift towards disposable tubular retractors to reduce the risk of infection and enhance efficiency in surgical settings.
