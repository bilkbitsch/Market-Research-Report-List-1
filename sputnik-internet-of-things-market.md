<p><p>The Sputnik Internet of Things (IoT) market comprises two primary types: backhaul services and direct-to-satellite services. Backhaul services focus on transmitting data from ground-based IoT devices to satellite networks, enabling efficient connectivity and data processing. In contrast, direct-to-satellite services provide IoT devices with direct communication to satellites, bypassing traditional terrestrial networks. This approach enhances coverage in remote areas, reduces latency, and supports various applications, from agricultural monitoring to supply chain management, improving the overall IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
