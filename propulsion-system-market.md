<p><ul><li>Air-Breathing</li><li>Non Air-Breathing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propulsion system market comprises two main types: air-breathing and non air-breathing systems. Air-breathing propulsion utilizes atmospheric oxygen for combustion, making it suitable for aircraft and missiles, as it is more efficient at lower altitudes. In contrast, non air-breathing systems, such as rocket engines, carry their oxidizers and fuel, enabling them to operate in the vacuum of space. Both systems play crucial roles in aerospace applications, catering to different operational requirements and performance characteristics.</p></p>
