<p><strong>The Medical Electric Endoscopic Stapler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Standard</li><li>Circular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical electric endoscopic stapler market is categorized into three main types: linear, standard, and circular staplers. Linear staplers are designed for straight-line cuts and multiple simultaneous firings, making them ideal for closing incisions in various procedures. Standard staplers are versatile tools used for general suturing tasks, providing secure tissue closure. Circular staplers, on the other hand, create circular rows of staples and are commonly used in surgeries requiring anastomosis, such as gastrointestinal procedures. Each type serves specific surgical needs.</p></p>
