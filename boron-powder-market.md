<p><p>The boron powder market is primarily divided into two types: amorphous and crystalline. Amorphous boron is characterized by its non-crystalline structure, offering unique properties suited for applications in fireworks and specialty glass. In contrast, crystalline boron features a well-defined arrangement of atoms, making it ideal for use in semiconductors, high-performance materials, and advanced ceramics. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing demand and pricing dynamics within the boron powder market.</p></p>
