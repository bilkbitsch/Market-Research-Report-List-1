<p><p>The CO2 heat pump market offers several types, including air to water monobloc systems, which integrate all components within a single unit for efficient installation. Air to water cylinder systems combine a heat pump with a separate hot water cylinder for better energy storage. Air to water split systems have separate indoor and outdoor units, enhancing flexibility and efficiency. Lastly, exhaust air heat pumps utilize warm air from buildings to generate heat, promoting energy recovery and sustainability in heating solutions.</p></p>
