<p><p>Plasticizers are chemical additives that enhance the flexibility, workability, and durability of plastics, particularly polyvinyl chloride (PVC). They lower the polymer's glass transition temperature, improving its processability and performance in various applications, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p><p>The Plasticizers Market is anticipated to grow at a CAGR of 0.95% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for flexible materials across multiple industries. A notable trend is the shift towards bio-based plasticizers due to rising environmental concerns and stricter regulatory frameworks. This shift aims to reduce dependency on traditional phthalate-based plasticizers, which have faced scrutiny for their potential health risks.</p><p>Moreover, the growing automotive sector is fuelling demand for lightweight materials that enhance fuel efficiency, further propelling plasticizer usage. The construction industry's recovery post-pandemic and increasing investments in infrastructure are also contributing to market growth.
