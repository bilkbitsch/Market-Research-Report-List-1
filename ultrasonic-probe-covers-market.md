<p><p>The ultrasonic probe covers market is categorized primarily into latex probe covers and latex-free probe covers. Latex probe covers are made from natural rubber latex, offering flexibility and a snug fit for various ultrasound probes. However, they may pose allergy risks for some patients. In contrast, latex-free probe covers are designed for individuals with latex sensitivities, utilizing alternative materials such as polyethene or polyurethane. Both types serve to maintain hygiene and protect probes during medical procedures, ensuring safe and effective imaging.</p></p>
