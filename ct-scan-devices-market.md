<p><ul><li>Low-slice Scanners</li><li>Medium-slice Scanners</li><li>High-slice Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CT scan devices market is categorized into three main types: low-slice, medium-slice, and high-slice scanners. Low-slice scanners typically handle 1-4 slices per rotation, providing basic imaging for straightforward diagnostics. Medium-slice scanners, with 16-64 slices, offer enhanced resolution and faster processing, making them suitable for more complex examinations. High-slice scanners, featuring 128 slices or more, deliver superior image quality and speed, enabling detailed 3D imaging and advanced applications, such as cardiac and oncology assessments, driving market growth.</p></p>
