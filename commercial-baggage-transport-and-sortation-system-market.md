<p><p>The Commercial Baggage Transport and Sortation System market comprises primarily two types: RFID Baggage Transport and Sortation Systems and Barcode Baggage Transport and Sortation Systems. RFID systems utilize radio frequency identification technology for real-time tracking and sorting of baggage, enhancing efficiency and reducing the risk of loss. Conversely, Barcode systems rely on printed barcodes scanned at various checkpoints, offering a reliable yet less advanced solution for baggage management. Both systems aim to streamline operations and improve the passenger experience.</p></p>
