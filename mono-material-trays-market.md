<p><p>The mono-material trays market encompasses three primary materials: Polypropylene (PP), Crystalline Polyethylene Terephthalate (CPET), and Polystyrene (PS). PP trays are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to moisture, making them ideal for food packaging. CPET trays offer excellent barrier properties and are suitable for high-temperature applications. PS trays, known for their rigidity and clarity, are often used for disposable food service items. Each material provides distinct advantages, catering to varied consumer preferences and sustainability initiatives in the packaging industry.</p></p>
